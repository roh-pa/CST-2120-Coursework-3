Restaurants

Las Tapas

The most famous mexican restaurant in Quatre Bornes selling the best burritos in the Indian Ocean

Mariana Bonita

Located in the capital of Mauritius, Mariana Bonita is always buzzing

Guac

A small, chic mexican restaurant situated in Mahebourg with one of the best fish tacos on the island

Dim Sum Express

Dim Sum Express sells enormous fried and steamed dumplings! One of the places you can't miss when coming to Vacoas.

Wonton Town

Wonton Town is a small, homely restaurant near the airport that sells, without doubt, the best ramen and wonton soup in the country. It is a must to go there!

Eighty Eight

Eighty Eight is one of the most popular chinese restaurants among tourists. It provides an ambient, romantic atmosphere while displaying their authentic chinese cuisines.

Sitar

Located not far from University of Mauritius, Sitar offers authentic North Indian food at an affordable price.

Pranam

If you are looking for pani puri and sev puri, Pranam is the right place!

Mayapore Cafe

Idli, sambar, dosa, come and discover pure south indian food at Mayapore Cafe in Quatre Bornes.

Rocomama

Come around for Rocomama's juicy lucy burgers and crinkled sweet potato fries!

Five Guys

The popular British food chain has opened its first branch in Mauritius! Five Guys is known for it's juicy beef burgers and best crispy french fries.

Burger Town

Another popular food spot in Tamarin attracting a lot of foodies for its aesthetic decor and crispy buttermilk chicken burgers.

Spur

When you think of a steakhouse in Mauritius, Spur is definitely the one that comes to your mind first. Spur offers high quality steak and ribs at an affordable price.

Savinia Bistrot

Chic, high-end restaurant near the airport. Indulge into rich dry-aged ribeyes and sirloins. Savia Bistrot is the number one steakhouse in Mauritius acccording to food critics!

The Irish

Not far from Plaza, The Irish is another popular and affordable steakhouse found in Rose-Hill.
